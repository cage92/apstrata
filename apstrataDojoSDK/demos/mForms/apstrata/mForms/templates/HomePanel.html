apstrata mForms

Welcome to apstrata mForms, the real-time Data Collection and Market Intelligence platform.

Apstrata mForms makes collecting real-time data from any target audience amazingly simple. Our forms can be pushed to mobile phones and through websites, social-networks and email. Campaign results arriving from all channels are consolidated in real-time into a single database.

The progress of the data-collection campaign can be monitored by looking at the real-time graphs provided in this application or by extracting the data to slice and dice in your favourite analysis tool.

Select an option from the left to create a new form, to launch a data-collection campaign or to monitor and extract your data.


Good luck with your next campaign, we hope you enjoy using mForms! And if you have any issues or need support, please let us know.
