Login to apstrata

This will capture your login credentials and use them to sign all the apstrata API requests sent by the application. You can look at how the requests are constructed and signed by moving your mouse over the REST monitor panel at the bottom of the page. You may change the apstrata service URL from the preferences panel.

If you use the Master Credentials that were provided when you created the apstrata account, this will give you superuser control over all API calls. You need to login in this mode to be able to create stores, groups, users, schemas and scripts.
